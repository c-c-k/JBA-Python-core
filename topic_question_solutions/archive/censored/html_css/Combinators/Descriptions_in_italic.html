Cheesecake

This cake is a healthy and low calorie desert. It goes well with your five-o-clock tea.

  • Graham crackers, butter
  • cream cheese, Greek yogurt
  • salt, sugar, vanilla extract
  • eggs

Blend crashed crackers and soft butter for the crust. Bake for 8 minutes. Use the other ingredients to make the filling. Spread it on the baked crust, bake the cake for 25 minutes.
